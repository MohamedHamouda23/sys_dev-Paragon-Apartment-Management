3-•	SMART – specific (clear deliverables – functioning system in tkinter), measurable (through evidence of working prototypes and gihub commits), attainable (python and tkinter are an appropriate means of development for the scale of work needed to complete this project), realistic (matches our project scope and outcomes), time bound (connect to implementation plan WEEK ETC)

4-•	SMART – specific (explicitly targets system validation testing requirements), measurable (though documented test results and bug reports), attainable (achievable methods of testing), realistic (proportionate to our project scale), time bound (connect to test plan and implementation plan WEEK ETC)  

5-•	SMART – specific (clearly addresses the LESP responsibilities), measurable (shown in documentation and compliance shown in code), attainable (existing frameworks can be used to compare and ensure our goals are being met), realistic (aligns with MO’s as well assessment brief), time bound (addressed throughout development, and connected to implementation plan WEEK ETC)

FR2 Managers

IDs	Requirement Description

FR2.2	View all tenant, apartment, and lease details within assigned city.
FR2.3	Generate performance, occupancy, and financial comparison reports for their city.

        FR5 Maintenance Staff
